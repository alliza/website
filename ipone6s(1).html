Iphone6s and Iphone6s plus launched

It is posted on 10-09-2015

Iphone 6s and 6s plus has launched in its annual event in Sans Francisco with new color added rose gold.It has same identical design though it has launched with new features and hardware upgrades. Here are some lists of features given below

  • 3d touch
  • 12-megapixel rear camera
  • 5-megapixel front-camera with flash light
  • Processor A9
  • IOS 9
  • Enhanced fingerprint security

    • 3d touch the pressure sensitive display.It allows you to press down the iphone screen to pull up the new menus and when press it down more it open the apps.Apple is calling "peek" and "pop" for the two way of interacting.Pop allows you to pull up overlays of photos and videos without actually having them take over the screen; once you move away, you'll be right back to where you were before.
